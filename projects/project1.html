TFT Team Builder

The TFT team builder was built to help new players figure out team comps for a game released by Riot Games called Teamfight Tactics. I was learning React.js and Redux and thought it would be cool if I could create something useful with it. This was my first and only time, so far, to build something with React.js, so as one might imagine, it's far from perfect, but it was the first online application that I was proud of and that actually looked like an online app.

The app is organized in 4 logical sections, a list of all champions, a list of champions in your team, a list of buffs you currently have, and a list of champions you need to complete specific buffs. You start off by selecting your team and then the application will suggest you which champions you need to complete specific buff. Champions can be added to your team either by selecting one from a list of all champions or by selecting a champion in suggested list. You can also remove champions from your current team simply by clicking on them. To see what certain buffs do just hover their icons. Website is hosted on Heroku
