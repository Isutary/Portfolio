XO

The TicTacToe game (or as it's called in my native language X-O) was a school project and was written in Python. We were tasked with creating a simple console-based game of TicTacToe, however, I wanted it to feel natural so I created the UI for it with the pythons turtle module. It was the first time ever I created something with the UI.

To play a game of TicTacToe compile x-o.py and you will be greeted with options to play against player or computer. If you select to play against the computer you will have the ability to select 2 difficulties. Easy mode will play random moves and hard mode will actively try to prevent you from winning and will try to win himself. If you select to play against other players, both players will take turns to play a move.
